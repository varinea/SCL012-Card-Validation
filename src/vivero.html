The Luhn Check is used to deterimine if a credit card is valid or not, not if it is active. The luhn alogrithim take every other number starting from the right and doubles them, then if any of those numbers are double digit, it seperates them so for example "12" would become "1", "2". It then takes all the digits and adds them up. If the credit card number is divisible by 10, you have valid credit card, Watch the video for better explanation of this.
